2: The Dice Game"


Description:

First of all. Two players should enter their names. The first player automatically will be the first one to start.
The dice should be rolled and whatever number does the dice fall in, the number adds up to his current score. The player can roll as much as he wants until if the rolled dice diplays 1.
If the rolled dice is 1, all current score of the player will be nullified and the turn will be of the second player and if the player decides to hold his score, player should press the 'Hold' button.
Then all the player's current score will be moved to the global score, which will not be downgraded if the dice is 1, and the next player's turn comes. The goal of the game is, whoever reaches 100 or more first wins the game
